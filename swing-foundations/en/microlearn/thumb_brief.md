## Typography Guidelines

### Primary Font
**Montserrat Bold** or **Inter Black** (modern, highly legible on mobile)

### Font Sizes (for 1080x1920)
- **Main headline**: 120-140px
- **Secondary text**: 60-80px
- **Small text/attribution**: 40-50px

### Text Effects
- **Outline**: 3-4px black stroke around white text
- **Shadow**: Subtle drop shadow for depth
- **Highlight**: Yellow (#FFD93D) underline for key words

---

## Production Specifications

### Technical Requirements
- **Format**: PNG or JPG (PNG preferred for text clarity)
- **Resolution**: 1080x1920 (vertical) or 1920x1080 (horizontal)
- **Color space**: sRGB
- **File size**: Under 2MB for fast loading

### Readability on Mobile
- Test thumbnail at 360x640 (small phone)
- Ensure text is readable without zooming
- High contrast between text and background
- Avoid thin fonts or small details

### Brand Consistency (Skola.dev)
- Include Skola.dev logo in bottom corner (subtle)
- Use Cabo Verde color accents (blue, yellow, green)
- Maintain professional, educational aesthetic
- Avoid cluttered or "clickbait" style

## Accessibility Considerations

### Color Blindness
- Ensure red/green contrast works for deuteranopia
- Use icons (X, checkmark) in addition to colors
- Test with color blindness simulator

### Text Contrast
- Minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratio (WCAG AA)
- White text on dark backgrounds
- Avoid text on busy background patterns

### Cultural Sensitivity
- Professional, welcoming tone
- Avoid aggressive or alarmist imagery
- Educational, not condescending
