# Transisan i Transformasan 3D

Botan muda kor na hover, ma é imediatu — flicker. Kards muda sombra, ma sentidu ka é klaru pamodi nada moveu. Transformasan + transisan é o ki ta da vida suavi: kards ki ta levanta, botan ki ta krese pikinu, ikon ki ta rota.

I v4 traze un kapasidadi grandi: **transformasan 3D nativu**. v3 era só 2D.

<SectionHeading variant="concept" seq={1}>Transformasan 2D — Movimentu Padran</SectionHeading>

### `translate-*` — Move

```html
<div class="translate-x-4">Moveu 1rem pa direita</div>
<div class="-translate-y-2">Moveu 0.5rem pa riba (negativu)</div>
<div class="translate-x-1/2">Moveu 50% pa direita (relativu a tamanho di elementu)</div>
```

### `scale-*` — Tamanhu

```html
<div class="scale-50">50% di tamanho</div>
<div class="scale-100">Padraun (sen muda)</div>
<div class="scale-110">110% — un poku más grandi</div>
<div class="scale-150">150% — grandi</div>
```

Valoris: 0, 50, 75, 90, 95, 100, 105, 110, 125, 150. Tambén `scale-x-*` i `scale-y-*` independenti.

### `rotate-*` — Rotasan

```html
<div class="rotate-3">3° (pikinu inklinasan)</div>
<div class="rotate-12">12°</div>
<div class="rotate-45">45°</div>
<div class="-rotate-45">-45° (kontráriu)</div>
<div class="rotate-180">180° (de kabesa)</div>
```

Valoris: 0, 1, 2, 3, 6, 12, 45, 90, 180.

### `skew-*` — Inklinasan

```html
<div class="skew-x-12">Inklinadu 12° na X</div>
<div class="skew-y-3">Inklinadu 3° na Y</div>
```

Otimu pa banners ku stilus dinámiku.

### `transform-origin-*` — Ponto di Rotasan

Default, transformasan ta rota na **sentru** di elementu. Pa muda:

```html
<div class="rotate-45 origin-top-left">Gira no kantu top-left</div>
<div class="rotate-45 origin-bottom-right">Gira no kantu bottom-right</div>
<div class="rotate-45 origin-center">Gira no centro (default)</div>
```

<SectionHeading variant="concept" seq={2}>Transformasan 3D — Novu na v4</SectionHeading>

v4 ta abri todu mundu di 3D ku CSS. v3 era só 2D — es é un kapasidadi ki so izisti na v4.

### `rotate-x-*` / `rotate-y-*` / `rotate-z-*`

```html
<div class="rotate-x-45">Rota 45° na eixu X (flip horizontal)</div>
<div class="rotate-y-45">Rota 45° na eixu Y (flip vertical)</div>
<div class="rotate-z-45">Rota 45° na eixu Z (= rotate normal)</div>
```

Otimu pa flipping kards, kards ki ta vira.

### `perspective-*` — Profundidadi 3D

Pa rotasan 3D ten sentidu vizual, pai meste ten `perspective`. Valoris: `perspective-near`, `perspective-normal`, `perspective-distant`, `perspective-none`, ou arbitráriu (`perspective-[1000px]`).

### `transform-3d`

Por padran, transformasan son **flat** — na 2D. Pa ativa 3D verdaderu, poi `transform-3d` na o elementu ki ta rota.

Es tres pesa — `perspective-*` na pai, `transform-3d` + `rotate-y-*` na fidju — ta djunta pa faze un flip-kard. Odja linha-pa-linha unde kada un ta bai:

<SectionHeading variant="concept" seq={3}>Transisan — Movimentu Suavi</SectionHeading>

Sen transisan, transformasan é imediatu. Adisiona transisan:

```html
<button class="hover:scale-110 transition">
  Botaun ki ta krese na hover
</button>
```

### Família di Klasis

```html
<button class="transition">Anima tudu</button>
<button class="transition-colors">Só kores</button>
<button class="transition-opacity">Só opasidadi</button>
<button class="transition-shadow">Só sombra</button>
<button class="transition-transform">Só transformasan (scale, rotate, translate, skew)</button>
<button class="transition-none">Sen transisan</button>
```

`transition-transform` é di klasi más uzadu pa hover effects (scale, rotate).

### `duration-*` — Tempu

```html
<button class="transition duration-75">75ms — rapidu</button>
<button class="transition duration-150">150ms (default)</button>
<button class="transition duration-300">300ms — suavi</button>
<button class="transition duration-700">700ms — lentu</button>
<button class="transition duration-1000">1000ms — dramátiku</button>
```

### `ease-*` — Ritmu di Velosidadi

```html
<button class="transition ease-linear">Linear — velosidadi konstanti</button>
<button class="transition ease-in">Ease-in — komesa devagar</button>
<button class="transition ease-out">Ease-out — termina devagar (más natural)</button>
<button class="transition ease-in-out">Ease-in-out — komesa i termina devagar</button>
```

`ease-out` é di más natural pa hover effects.

<SectionHeading variant="concept" seq={4}>Padrons di Hover i Click</SectionHeading>

### Kart "Lift" na Hover

```html
<div class="bg-white p-6 rounded-lg shadow-sm hover:scale-105 hover:shadow-lg transition duration-200 ease-out">
  Karta ki ta levanta-se na hover
</div>
```

Es kombina: `scale-105` (5% más grandi), `shadow-lg` (sombra major), `transition duration-200 ease-out`. Resultadu: karta ki ta parese vivu sob rato.

### Boton "Click"

```html
<button class="px-4 py-2 bg-sky-600 text-white rounded-md hover:bg-sky-700 hover:scale-105 active:scale-95 transition duration-150">
  Klika
</button>
```

`active:scale-95` ta da feedback di klike (boton **diminui pikinu** kuandu apertadu) — sensasan di material.
